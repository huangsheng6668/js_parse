Bitstamp goes live on BLINC – first exchange to enable instant and free EUR and GBP transfers

26 AUG 2020
After previously announcing we would serve as the launch partner for BLINC, a new global payments network that allows institutions to transfer funds instantly 24/7/365, Bitstamp has now gone live on the network.

Our customers who are also members of BLINC can now transfer EUR and GBP to and from their Bitstamp accounts instantly and with zero transaction fees. This greatly improves transaction efficiency for our institutional customers, further strengthening the bridge between crypto and traditional finance.

BLINC was created by BCB Group, a leading digital asset financial services group, to directly address settlement inefficiencies in global payment systems. BLINC currently covers transfers of fiat currencies, with plans to expand into transfers of digital assets as well. It is the first real-time settlement network focused on the cryptocurrency industry in Europe.

As both the world’s longest-running crypto exchange and the biggest one in Europe, we are excited about becoming the first exchange to integrate with BLINC and supporting BCB Group’s efforts to create solutions that enable more institutional adoption of cryptocurrencies.

Temporary change to our ETH withdrawal fees

14 AUG 2020
Lately, Ethereum has been seeing a lot of activity on the blockchain, leading to network congestion and a significant increase in transaction fees. Because of this, we are temporarily increasing Bitstamp’s withdrawal fees for ETH to 0.02 ETH per withdrawal, to reflect the increased cost of Ethereum transactions.

We are monitoring the situation closely and will continue to adapt our withdrawal fees as the network congestion changes, with the goal of returning to our standard fee schedule as soon as possible. Thank you for your understanding and continued support in this matter.

Bitstamp becomes the launch partner for BLINC by BCB Group

27 JUL 2020
We’re excited to announce Bitstamp will become the first cryptocurrency exchange to enable transactions on BLINC, a new settlement network by BCB Group that enables members to move fiat and crypto between accounts instantly, 24/7 and with zero transaction fees.

The BLINC network enables institutions to forgo conventional payment systems such as SWIFT and SEPA in favor of an architecture that supports free-of-charge instant transactions between members of the network. Transactions in all supported currencies are settled and cleared instantly, enabling funds to flow seamlessly between members of BLINC.

Bitstamp’s integration with the settlement network is currently underway and will be completed over the following months. By supporting BLINC, we are offering our institutional customers a frictionless mechanism to fund their trading accounts using GBP and EUR, giving traders at Bitstamp an edge on the fast-moving crypto markets and taking an important step towards strengthening the bridge between crypto and traditional finance.

We see improved efficiency of transactions as one of the key drivers of institutional adoption and are excited to expand the availability of these services at Bitstamp. Our exchange was already integrated with Fireblocks for faster crypto transactions as well as Signet from Signature Bank and SEN from Silvergate Bank for real-time USD transactions. The addition of BLINC expands the availability of these services to more customers and more currencies.

Bitstamp supports the launch of PayID to simplify global payments

18 JUN 2020
One of our main goals at Bitstamp has always been to give our customers the freedom to control what forms of money they use and to transfer it easily to anyone in the world. We’ve now joined a coalition of more than 40 leading companies across tech, finance and the non-profit sector to launch PayID – a new standard for global payments that makes sending money as simple as sending an email.

PayID can be enabled by any business that sends or receives money — whether a bank, payment provider or processor, digital wallet, or remittance provider. Implementing PayID makes it possible for businesses to access more networks, consumers, and currencies to expand their businesses globally.

With PayID, people have a simple ID that’s easy to remember - similar to an email address - so they can send money to friends and family, not a routing number. PayID allows you to send and receive money across any payment network, in any currency. It is a free solution that is simple to integrate and open for anyone to use.

As part of the 40+ company Open Payments Coalition behind the new service, Bitstamp joins a pool of businesses that reaches over 100 million consumers worldwide. We believe PayID presents an exciting opportunity to break down the barriers that currently exist in payments and change the way money is sent around the world. Cryptocurrencies have always followed that goal and now we’re embracing this chance to further strengthen our connection with traditional finance, to the mutual benefit of both businesses and consumers worldwide. For more information, go to www.payid.org.

Introducing Stellar Lumens (XLM) and Paxos Standard (PAX) at Bitstamp with zero fees until September!

1 JUN 2020
We’re excited to add support for Stellar Lumens (XLM), one of the oldest active cryptocurrencies, and Paxos Standard (PAX), our first stablecoin!

Both new assets will be paired with USD, EUR, BTC and GBP. PAX will also be paired with XRP and ETH (full list below). All new pairs will have no trading fees until 1 September 2020!

We’re adding XLM and PAX trading in the following stages:

1. Transfer-only mode: Deposits and withdrawals open but trading is not enabled yet.
PAX: Monday, 8 June 2020
XLM: Monday, 15 June 2020

2. Post-only mode: You will be able to place and cancel limit orders, but they will not be matched. Therefore, no orders will actually be completed during this stage.
PAX: Wednesday, 10 June at 7:00 AM UTC
XLM: Wednesday, 17 June at 7:00 AM UTC

3. Limit-only mode: Order matching will start and trades will be executed normally. However, only limit orders will be available.
PAX: Wednesday, 10 June at 11:00 AM UTC
XLM: Wednesday, 17 June at 11:00 AM UTC

4. Full trading: Once the order books gather sufficient liquidity, we will enable all order types and card purchases for the new trading pairs as well.

Follow us on Twitter for updates on the roll out of the new XLM and PAX pairs.

Log in to your Bitstamp account to start trading and get the Bitstamp mobile app to trade all of our cryptocurrencies on the go!

Here is a full list of the PAX and XLM trading pairs we’re adding:

• PAX/USD
• PAX/EUR
• PAX/GBP
• BTC/PAX
• ETH/PAX
• XRP/PAX
• XLM/BTC
• XLM/USD
• XLM/EUR
• XLM/GBP

About XLM:
Stellar Lumens (XLM) are tokens built on Stellar’s decentralized, open-source protocol, which allows digital representations of any form of money, from BTC to USD or EUR, to be transferred cross-border with negligible fees. XLM is Stellar’s native currency token.

About PAX:
Paxos Standard (PAX) is a stablecoin pegged to the US dollar and issued by the Paxos Trust Company. PAX’s price stability is ensured through 1:1 backing with USD. It is built on the Ethereum blockchain, following the ERC-20 protocol.

These assets have successfully completed our internal review process, making them eligible for listing. We are reviewing a number of additional assets and will keep you updated as new listings are confirmed.

Introducing GBP trading with zero fees for first three months!

27 MAY 2020
We’re excited to announce the pound sterling is coming to Bitstamp next week! All of our fiat and cryptocurrencies will get an additional GBP pair, with zero trading fees through 1 September 2020. This includes USD, EUR, BTC, ETH, XRP, LTC and BCH.

We’re adding GBP trading in the following stages:

1. Transfer-only mode: GBP deposits and withdrawals open on 1 June, but trading will not be enabled yet.

2. Post-only mode: Starting at 7 am UTC on 3 June, you will be able to place and cancel limit orders, but they will not be matched. Therefore, no orders will actually be completed during this stage.

3. Limit-only mode: At 9 AM UTC on 3 June, order matching will start and trades will be executed normally. However, only limit orders will be available.

4. Full trading: Once the order books gather sufficient liquidity, we will enable all order types for the new trading pairs as well.

GBP trading pairs will be available worldwide, except in certain US states.

More new features are coming soon. Log in to your Bitstamp account to see our redesigned platform and download the Bitstamp mobile app for when you’re on the go!
